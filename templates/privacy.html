Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 21, 2026

1. Data Governance

The Atmo Sync Carbon Footprint Awareness Platform is built with privacy at its core. **We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data** entered in the carbon calculator to our servers or any third-party databases.

All inputs, sliders, select values, and resulting calculations are processed **locally inside your web browser**.

2. Cookies and Storage

This website does not use tracking cookies. We do not store session tokens or track your browsing activity across the web.

3. External Connections

Our application acts as a proxy to fetch live electricity grid carbon intensity data from the official, open-source National Energy System Operator (NESO) API. No identifiable personal information of yours is sent to the NESO servers.

4. Contact

If you have any questions regarding these privacy practices, please contact the developer via the links in the footer.
